The Computational & Structural Biology Group at EEAD-CSIC has interest in the following research topics:

  • (Pan)genomics and transcriptional regulation of grasses
  • Evolutionary and structural analysis of ductile proteins (IDPs) and flavoenzymes

The group is part of the INB/ELIXIR ES node and Conexin BCB CSIC.
